IPA English Mastery

Want to improve your English pronunciation? IPA English helps you learn and master the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA), making pronunciation easier and more accurate.

★★★★★
5.0 • 2 Ratings

Features

  • Complete IPA Chart – Learn phonetic symbols with clear explanations and audio examples.
  • Audio Pronunciation Guide – Hear native-like pronunciation for each IPA symbol.
  • Word & Sentence Practice – Improve your pronunciation with real-world examples.
  • Interactive Quizzes – Test your knowledge and track your progress.
  • Minimal Pairs Training – Distinguish similar sounds to reduce mispronunciation.

Who is this app for?

  • English learners who want to improve their pronunciation.
  • Teachers & linguists looking for a handy IPA reference.
  • Anyone interested in phonetics and clear communication.
